# AI-Follow-up-System
It is an automatic customer follow-up system.
## Problem
Most of the bussinesses follow up their customers manually, It wastes lot of time.
## Solution
As a solution I built an Automated AI Follow Up System. It takes lead from website, updates the CRM and follow up the customer through professional Email generated by Gemini AI.
## Workflow
Form Submission(in website) → Google Sheets (CRM) → Gemini AI → Email → Google Sheets (Update CRM)
## Features
• Append and Update CRM from Lead .

• Follow-Up the customer with in a day.

• Sends a proper individual professional email for every customer.

• Search for Email existance in CRM sheet to avoid duplicates.
## Tech Used 
• n8n

• Webhooks

• Google Sheets

• Gemini AI
